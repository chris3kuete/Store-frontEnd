ABOUT US

Brand that was created by Anaclet Kuete back in 2010 and was first named Built For Success. Later on, due to the fact that Anaclet Kuete thought the name Built For Success didn't really represent him, he changed it to Strive Athletic. But later on, in 2016, Anaclet Kuete tragically passed away due to a sudden Heart Attack. His brother, who was with him at the moment he passed away, decided to continue with the brand to honor his brother and keep thinking of him since that is the only thing Anaclet left us that can help us not forget him. Also, Anaclet before his sudden passed away, had discuss with his brother Chris about his goals for the brand. Chris would like to make sure that those goals he was trying to achieve for his brand are achieved to honor him and make him proud from wherever he is.

MISSION

To Make sure everybody is able to afford a quality Strive T-shirt

VISION

Driven by our passion for sport and our instinct for innovation, we aim to bring inspiration to every athlete in the world and to make sport a daily habit.
